About Us

United Minerals Group LLC specializes in representing various mining companies, metal producers and chemical companies worldwide. UMG’s marketing strength is to develop and open markets for various raw material producers and act as a producer’s agent, with complete transparency between the supplier and consumer, to create effective long term supply relationships.

UMG is supported by several of the world’s most important raw material suppliers and mining companies that allows consumers to have the ability to rely on long term supplies. Along with strategic partners such as Stork International, Vienna and others, UMG can offer a range of creative financial and logistic services that assist both the mining companies and consumers to execute contracts in an efficient and effective way.

We have listed in our website the various partners UMG represents and the raw materials that they produce. Please contact us with your business inquires.
